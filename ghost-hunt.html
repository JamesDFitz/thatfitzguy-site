Ghost Hunt

Control Blinky and coordinate with the squad to catch the orb-hungry runner before it clears the maze.

Level: 1 Orbs Left: 0 Runner Escapes: 0/3
How to play
  • You are the red ghost. Use Arrow keys / WASD.
  • Blue, pink, and orange ghosts are AI teammates.
  • Catch the runner before all orbs are collected.
  • Each new maze gives the runner smarter, faster routes.
